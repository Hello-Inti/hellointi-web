What Does "helloInti" Mean?

The story behind our name reveals everything about our mission.

"Inti" is the Quechua word for sun — the life-giving force that connects all living things. Just as the sun creates the conditions for life to flourish, we create the conditions for human connection to flourish.

"Hello" is the simplest, most universal invitation to connection. It's the first word that opens every meaningful relationship.

Together, "helloInti" represents our belief that every greeting has the potential to spark something life-giving — a collaboration, a movement, a transformation.
