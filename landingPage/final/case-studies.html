Real Impact Stories

Discover how organizations are transforming their communities through the power of peer-to-peer connection.

I Ching with Clarity

For students in an online learning environment, creating a sense of community can be a challenge. I Ching with Clarity used our peer-to-peer matching system to connect students for weekly practice sessions. The result was a vibrant and supportive community that extended far beyond the classroom. Students formed lasting friendships, organized their own study groups, and even met up in person, transforming an online course into a thriving community of practice.

“Some sort of magic happened in the group as a whole: both those groups developed friendships and a powerful sense of community that lasted long after the class finished.”

Context Institute

The Bright Future Network, a community of changemakers at the Context Institute, used our system to build cross-connections and foster a sense of community warmth. Our platform provided an easy and time-efficient way for members to stay engaged, even those who were less active in other community activities. The result was a more connected and resilient network, where members felt a stronger sense of belonging and were more likely to collaborate on new initiatives.

“A great time-efficient and fun way to build lots of cross-connections in a community...it nourishes a wonderful feeling of community warmth.”

Seeds DAO

In a large, decentralized online community like SEEDS, it can be difficult for new members to find their place. We implemented our peer-to-peer matching system to provide an easy entry point for new members, helping them get their questions answered and feel a sense of belonging. The program became a huge attraction for the entire network, with members referring to it as emblematic of the community's wellbeing and longevity.

“As a community builder and steward I was surprised and delighted to see how the program took a life of its own and it became a huge attraction for the whole network.”

Awaken Tango

For the Awaken Tango community, connection and collaboration are at the heart of their work. Our system provided a fun, easy, and effective way to bring members into contact with each other, giving a light structure and prompts to their conversations. This fostered a more generative and collaborative environment, strengthening the network and helping members build deeper connections with each other.

“Giving a light structure and prompts to inner-network conversations was a useful and generative tool for our network.”
