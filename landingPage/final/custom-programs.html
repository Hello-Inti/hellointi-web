Custom Programs Information

Beyond the 11-Week Program

Our core Pairathon program is a powerful tool for building connection, but we understand that every community is unique. That's why we offer custom-designed programs that adapt our peer-to-peer activation system to your specific context, goals, and timeline. Whether you're a brand looking to engage your customers, a celebrity wanting to connect with your fans, or a common interest group seeking to deepen your community, we can create a program that's right for you.

Endless Possibilities

Our custom programs are designed to be flexible and adaptable. Here are just a few examples of how we can tailor our system to your needs:

  • Brand Communities: Foster a deeper connection between your customers and your brand by creating a space for them to connect with each other.
  • Celebrity & Fan Engagement: Create a unique and intimate experience for your fans by allowing them to connect with each other in a structured and meaningful way.
  • Common Interest Groups: Whether you're a society of plant medicine facilitators or a group of vintage car enthusiasts, we can help you build a more connected and engaged community.
  • Corporate Teams: Break down silos and foster cross-departmental collaboration with a custom-designed internal Pairathon program.

Flexible & Adaptable

Our custom programs are not bound by the 11-week timeline of our standard Pairathon. We can design programs that run for as long or as short as you need, from a weekend-long intensive to a year-long community-building initiative. We'll work with you to understand your goals and design a program that meets your specific needs and budget.
