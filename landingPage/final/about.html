About hello Inti

We create the conditions for human connection to flourish, transforming networks into living ecosystems.

What Does "hello Inti" Mean?

"Inti" is a beautiful ancient word that holds many meanings related to the sun: radiance, solar energy, and divinity. It was introduced to us in a sacred vision as a way of naming human potential.

"Hello" is the simplest, most universal greeting, the doorway to the future. It is a word of beginning that represents an intent to connect, to lean in, to create meaning through relationship.

"helloInti" invites us to see the brightness in each other as we welcome the mysterious potential of a chance-based conversation with a fellow human. It represents our belief that every greeting has the potential to spark something life-giving, a collaboration, a movement, a transformation.

Through this practice, the social body—your community or network—becomes like a hand raised in welcome, opening up to the future. Each member is like fingers of a hand, connected yet individual, ready to co-create the new and necessary.

We are in deep gratitude for the opportunity to carry this work to the brave ecosystem leaders stewarding decentralized networks and systems change.
